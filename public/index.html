Welcome to 3DWE, the hub for 3D printing enthusiasts

This site is for you, the enthusiast, the curious experimentalist. Here you will find resources to get started in 3D printing, a 3D printing network, and a members area that is soon to be launched. About

3DWE was started to encourage collaboration in the 3D printing domain. Our desire is for every person to take an interest in 3D printing, to print for themselves and to print for others, should they choose. YOUR MISSION, SHOULD YOU CHOOSE TO ACCEPT IT, IS TO IMMERSE YOURSELF IN YOUR DEVELOPMENT, TO LEARN, TO GROW AND GAIN CONFIDENCE IN THIS DOMAIN. Here's to the Maverick's, here's to you.

Resources

Check out our community projects. Here will completed projects by fellow enthusiasts who have printed some interesting designs. Learn from them, try out their projects, then build your own. Below is a Batman model printed by Karthik Rajasekar from Uppsala University.

The settings for this print have been shared in a newsletter, so please sign up to get started. Below are additional settings needed to print the model.

1. Download Cura 3D printing slicer at Ultimaker: https://ultimaker.com/software/ultimaker-cura

2. Download the Batman file at thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:241899/files

3. Print speed set to 30 mm/s

4. Intial layer speed set to 60mm/s

5. Set print cooling on if possible and fan speed to full blast.

6. Enable support to be generated and specify "everywhere".

7. Support overhang angle can be set to 50.

8. Set build plate adhesion to Brim and choose 4 for the Brim line count.

9. You can play with these settings, but these have given success before.

Good luck and happy printing!
